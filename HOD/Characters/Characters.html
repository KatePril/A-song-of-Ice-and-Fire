Rhaenyra Targaryen

the first-born child of King Viserys I Targaryen. Rhaenyra is King Viserys I's only living child by his first wife, Queen Aemma of House Arryn.

Daemon Targaryen

is a member of House Targaryen, being a younger son of Prince Baelon Targaryen and a brother of King Viserys I Targaryen.

Alicent Hightower

is a member of House Hightower who became the second wife to King Viserys I Targaryen.

Otto Hightower

is a knight from House Hightower who served as Hand of the King to three kings, although two of the kings removed him from office at various times

Rhaenys Targaryen

is the daughter of Prince Aemon Targaryen and Lady Jocelyn Baratheon. She was married to Lord Corlys Velaryon of Driftmark.

Corlys Velaryon

known as the Sea Snake, is a fabled Lord of the Tides, Master of Driftmark, and head of House Velaryon.

Viserys I Targaryen

was the fifth Targaryen king to sit the Iron Throne, ruling from 103 AC to 129 AC. He succeeded his grandfather, the Old King Jaehaerys I.

Criston Cole

is a knight from House Cole who rose to become Lord Commander of the Kingsguard for Viserys I Targaryen.
